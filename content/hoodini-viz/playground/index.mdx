## Quick Start

Choose `HoodiniDashboard` or `HoodiniViz` based on your needs:

| Component | Best For | Includes |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| **HoodiniDashboard** | Most users | Data loading, sidebar controls, full UI |
| **HoodiniViz** | Advanced users | Visualization only, you manage data & UI |
